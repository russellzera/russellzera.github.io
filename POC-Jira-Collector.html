HCD IT Infrastructure & Operations (ITIO) Strategic Intake

Welcome to the Health Care Delivery IT Infrastructure & Operations (HCD-ITIO) Intake Portal. The HCD-ITIO team partners with leaders across CVS, HCD, and broader enterprise groups to architect, execute, and deliver major infrastructure and operational initiatives.

What is this portal for? This request path is reserved for project-level initiatives that require HCD-ITIO engineering, architecture, or service delivery resources (e.g., new infrastructure stand-ups, major system integrations, enterprise security alignments).

Submit Your Request Here If:

  • Your project requires HCD-ITIO engineering, architecture, or service delivery team support
  • You are initiating a new infrastructure stand-up or cloud environment
  • You are managing major system integrations across departments
  • You need HCD security alignment for a new initiative
  • Your request requires leadership alignment on Business Value and individual or team bandwidth

Do NOT Use This Form If:

  • You need standard IT support, such as password resets or MFA help.
  • You have hardware issues, including laptop repairs or monitor replacements
  • You are experiencing local software issues or need application access

If your need aligns with the “Do NOT Use This Form If” list, please route your request through the standard CVS General Helpdesk.

How the Process Works & What to Expect

To ensure successful execution and protect enterprise capacity, all requests follow a structured engagement model:

  1. 1. Submit Your Request: Click the link below and provide the core details (Who you are, your department, a brief summary of your request, and your email address).
  2. 2. Clarification & Scoping: Within 2 Business Days, a member of the IT Service Delivery (ITSD) team will contact you directly. We will work with you to clarify the exact technical boundaries, understand the core business requirement, and define the true scope of the work.
  3. 3. Strategic Prioritization & Timeline Formulation: We prioritize execution based on enterprise impact and roadmap alignment. Once the technical scope is clarified, we will evaluate your request against our active ITIO portfolio. This vital step ensures we can secure the appropriate engineering bandwidth and partner with you to establish a realistic, committed delivery timeline.
  4. 4. Leadership Alignment: For macro-level projects, ITSD will coordinate a final sync between your leadership and ITIO leadership to formally agree on the scope, timeline, and Business Value before it is locked into our active execution queue.
