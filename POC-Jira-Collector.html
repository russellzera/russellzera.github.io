HCD-IT Infrastructure & Operations (ITIO) Service Intake

Welcome to the Health Care Delivery - IT Infrastructure & Operations (HCD-ITIO) Service Intake Portal. The ITIO team partners with leaders across CVS, HCD, and broader enterprise groups to architect, execute, and deliver major infrastructure and operational initiatives.

What is this portal for?

This portal is reserved for project-level initiatives that require ITIO engineering, architecture, or service delivery resources (e.g., new infrastructure stand-ups, major system integrations, enterprise security alignments).

Note: For standard IT support (e.g., password resets, hardware repairs, local software issues), please route your request through the standard CVS General Helpdesk.

How the Process Works

  1. Submit Your Request: Click the link below to provide high-level details about your initiative (Who you are, your department, and the core request).
  2. ITIO Triage: The IT Service Delivery (ITSD) team will review the request for scope and initial feasibility.
  3. Leadership Alignment: For project-level initiatives, ITSD will coordinate a sync between your team's leadership and ITIO leadership to define scope, timeline, and business value before formal roadmap integration.
